Hello !

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to direct your brightly coloured space aliens to their food.

Do this by clicking on each alien and moving them with WASD. But remember that the center of your vision will always be the fire alien, so make sure you do not lose the others! If you run out of life on any of aliens, the game will be over! So don't neglect anyone. The game ends when you eat all the food.
